What is Suncorp Frequent Learners?

SFL is intended to build a collobrative learning environment where sharing and learning is a natural part of your work

How does SFL foster a learning environment?

SFL will reward frequent learning through badges ' points to demostrate progress and to track your learning activities against your peers, irrespective of your role
