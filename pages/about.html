How is gamification part of Suncorp Frequent Learners?

Suncorp Frequent Learners intends to make learning fun by using elements of gamification to encourage collabative sharing and learning


Suncorp Frequent Learners will intergrate with available 3rd party systems to pull content and information, and collate into a central view of the world to advise the learning opportunities that are available.


The use of data from these external tools can also feed into to a game engine to track achievements and provide metrics around people's learning styles and behaviours, and provide reward and encouragement for those that share and learn with their peers within and external to suncorp.
